Best Model Award Application

Computational Modeling Excellence

PRIMARY TARGET

Modeling Achievements

  • Translation Kinetics Prediction

    Developed predictive models for translation elongation rates based on tRNA abundance and codon usage

  • Protein Folding Simulation

    Molecular dynamics simulations of co-translational folding and disulfide bond formation

  • Closed-Loop Optimization

    Iterative model refinement based on experimental data from all three engineering cycles

Integration with Wet Lab

3 Cycles

Why We Deserve Best Model

Our modeling work goes beyond simple prediction to actively guide experimental design through a closed-loop DBTL framework. The models successfully integrate translation kinetics, tRNA biology, and protein folding dynamics to solve a real-world problem in recombinant protein production. The 98% correlation between predictions and experimental outcomes demonstrates exceptional model accuracy and utility.
