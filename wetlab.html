Experimental Protocols

01

Cycle 1: tRNA Engineering Experiments

tRNA Gene Modification

  • λ-Red homologous recombination for precise tRNA gene deletion
  • CRISPR-Cas9-mediated deletion as alternative approach
  • Targeted tRNA families: Arg, Leu, Gly

Functional Characterization

  • tRNA abundance measurement: qRT-PCR and tRNA-seq
  • Growth curve analysis (OD600 measurements)
  • Translation efficiency reporter assays

Short-Peptide Reporter System

Constructed plasmid with codon-enriched short peptide sequences to assess translation efficiency changes in engineered strains.

pET vector system His-tag detection T7 promoter
02

Cycle 2: Signal Peptide & Disulfide Bond Experiments

Signal Peptide Optimization

  • Molecular simulation screening of signal peptide sequences
  • Synonymous codon variants for translation speed control
  • Three variants: slow, medium, fast translation rates

Disulfide Bond Analysis

  • BPTI (bovine pancreatic trypsin inhibitor) as model protein
  • Molecular dynamics simulations of nascent chain folding
  • Key position identification for codon substitution

Expression Vector Construction

  • pET-22b plasmid with lac/T7-lac inducible promoter
  • Expression cassette: Promoter - Signal peptide - BPTI - His-tag - GroEL
  • GroEL as internal reference for expression normalization

Experimental Controls

Control 1
BPTI + wild-type E. coli
Control 2
Signal peptide + BPTI + wild-type E. coli
Control 3
Optimized BPTI + Cycle 1 chassis (no signal peptide)
03

Cycle 3: Scytovirin Expression Experiments

SVN Protein Characterization

Scytovirin (SVN) is a cyanobacterial antiviral protein containing five disulfide bonds, representing a structurally complex validation target.

Each disulfide bond region (8-15 amino acids surrounding bond sites) is analyzed separately to identify translation speed requirements.

Synonymous codon variants (low/medium/high speed) constructed for each critical region.

Testing Methods

Native-PAGE Analysis

Qualitative detection of proper disulfide bond formation and folding state

ELISA Plate Analysis

Quantitative measurement of functional activity and expression levels

Disulfide Bond Testing Strategy

1
First Disulfide Bond Test

Identify key positions through MD simulations, construct three speed variants, determine optimal translation rhythm

2-5
Subsequent Bonds (2-5)

Apply same methodology to remaining disulfide bonds, integrate optimal strategies into final SVN sequence
