PROJECT BACKGROUND

Why translation kinetics matter

Protein folding is tightly coupled to the rate at which ribosomes translate mRNA. By adjusting translation speed through codon and tRNA engineering, we can influence co-translational folding pathways and improve yield and function of complex proteins.

Use the interactive widget to explore how translation rate affects folding: slow translation allows orderly folding, fast translation can create misfolded aggregates.
