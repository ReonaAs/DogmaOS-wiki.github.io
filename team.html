Organizational Principles

Clear Role Definition & Specialization

Pre-project skills survey to align tasks with individual strengths (PCR expertise, Python coding, graphic design, science communication)

Transparent Responsibility Matrix

Visual RACI charts defining who is in charge of each part, who approves completed work, and who needs updates

Balanced Workload & Flexibility

Fair workload distribution with built-in flexibility for task swapping and peer support

Documentation & Knowledge Sharing

Shared drive/wiki with clear naming conventions and version control for all project documentation

Collaboration Mechanisms

Cross-Group Division of Labor

Structured interdisciplinary task collaboration ensuring all teams work together efficiently

New Member Integration

Mentor-guided reporting system and paired workshops for rapid onboarding

Regular Team Meetings

Weekly team-wide meetings and pair-based learning framework in wet lab

Decision-Making Framework

Brainstorming Workshops

Regular team workshops for interdisciplinary discussions and creative problem-solving

HP Involvement

Human Practices team involvement in all wet lab decisions to ensure ethical considerations

Advisory Role

Former team members and experienced faculty provide guidance and oversight
